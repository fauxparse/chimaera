## Ranged sliders

To make a ranged slider, pass a two-element `[number, number]` tuple to the `value` prop.
Your `onChange` handler must also accept a value of the same type.

> The values passed to `onChange` will always be in order,
> regardless of whether the thumbs have switched positions.
